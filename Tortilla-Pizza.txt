### Tortilla Pizza Recipe

#### Ingredients
- 2 large flour tortillas
- 1/2 cup pizza sauce (store-bought or homemade)
- 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
- 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 1/2 cup pepperoni slices (or any other preferred toppings)
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- Olive oil
- Fresh basil leaves (optional, for garnish)

#### Instructions

**1. Preheat the Oven:**
1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).

**2. Prepare the Tortillas:**
1. Lightly brush both sides of each tortilla with olive oil.
2. Place the tortillas on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.

**3. Assemble the Tortilla Pizzas:**
1. Spread a thin layer of pizza sauce over each tortilla, leaving a small border around the edges.
2. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce.
3. Add pepperoni slices or your preferred toppings.
4. Sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese and dried oregano over the top.

**4. Bake the Tortilla Pizzas:**
1. Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven.
2. Bake for 8-10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly, and the edges of the tortillas are golden and crispy.

**5. Serve:**
1. Remove the tortilla pizzas from the oven and let them cool for a minute.
2. Garnish with fresh basil leaves if desired.
3. Slice and serve immediately.
